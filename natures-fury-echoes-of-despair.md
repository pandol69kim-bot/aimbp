# Nature's Fury: Echoes of Despair

**Genre:** Rock
**Mood:** 어둡고 강렬한
**Theme:** 환경 파괴와 자연의 분노

---

## Verse

Sky's on fire, the world's gone mad,
In a storm of tears, the earth feels sad.
El Niño's rage, no mercy, no end,
Fields of dust where rivers once did bend.

A desert breathes where green once grew,
Cracked soil speaks of promises untrue.
Hearts as dry as the endless drought,
In silence, we scream, we shout.

---

## Chorus

Cry the storm, scream the pain,
We're lost in the hurricane.
Cry the storm, feel the flame,
The earth's not playing your game.

Sky's on fire, hear us loud,
We rage beneath this ruthless cloud.
Cry the storm, face the fear,
The earth's loud message, crystal clear.

---

## Bridge

We chase the wind, shadows of the past,
Praying for mercy that never lasts.
The earth's own voice, a grieving song,
A warning to right what we've done wrong.

The rain's a rebel, floods the land,
Drowning dreams where we stand.
The skies won't listen, thunder's roar,
Tidal waves crash on the shore.

---

## Hook

Is there a way to heal this plight?
In nature's wrath, we fight the night.
Is there a way to calm this ire?
In nature's fury, we walk on fire.
